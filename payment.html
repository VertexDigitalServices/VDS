Payment Policy

Vertex Digital Services follows a transparent and agreed-upon payment process for all projects.

Payments are typically collected in advance or in milestones, depending on the project size and scope.

The project will begin only after confirmation of the initial payment. Remaining payments must be cleared before final delivery of the project unless otherwise agreed in writing.

All payment details, including total cost and milestones, will be clearly communicated before starting the project.

Late payments may result in delays in project delivery. Vertex Digital Services is not responsible for delays caused due to pending payments.
