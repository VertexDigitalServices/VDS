Refund Policy

At Vertex Digital Services, we aim to provide high-quality digital services and complete customer satisfaction. Since our services involve customized digital work, refunds are handled under specific conditions.

Refund requests must be made within a reasonable time after project initiation or payment. Refunds may be considered only if no significant work has been started on the project.

Once development or design work has begun, refunds will generally not be applicable due to the time and effort already invested.

If a project is canceled by the client after work has started, charges may apply based on the work completed up to that point.

Any disputes regarding refunds will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Our goal is always to resolve issues fairly and professionally.
